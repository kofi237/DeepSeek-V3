Camera Distance Measure

Use your phone camera to estimate the distance to an object by marking its top and bottom in the captured frame.

Default is 60°. You can calibrate this value using a known distance.

Tap or click on the captured frame to mark the bottom and top of the object.

Estimated Distance

–

Distance is computed from the relative height of the object in the frame and the vertical field of view.

Tips

  • Stand still after capturing the frame to avoid motion blur.
  • Measure an object whose actual height you know precisely.
  • Calibrate the FOV by measuring a known distance and adjusting the value until it matches.
